``` r
survey_clean <- survey
```

For most purposes we can consider “prefer not to answer” and “NA” (did
not answer) to be the same. The “Other or prefer not to answer”
observations are more tricky, because there is no way to distinguish
between survey respondents who identify with a gender that is not male,
female or non-binary and those that preferred not to answer. For our
purposes, it will be sufficient to group all three responses together as
“Other or prefer not to answer.”

``` r
survey_clean <- survey_clean %>%
  mutate(gender_clean = str_replace_all(gender, "Prefer not to answer", "Other or prefer not to answer")) # %>%
#  select(-gender)

survey_clean %>% 
  group_by(gender_clean) %>%
  summarize(n = n())
```
